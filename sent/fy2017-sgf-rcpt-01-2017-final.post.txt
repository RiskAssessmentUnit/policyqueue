FY 2017 State General Fund Receipts

In FY 2017, the State of Kansas received a total of $3,345,709,800 in cumulative actual revenue. This included $1,378,801,102 from individual income taxes and $45,799,871 from corporate income taxes.

Miscellaneous taxes, which include corporate franchise taxes and fees, made up a significant portion of the state's revenue, as noted by the Kansas Legislature's Office of the Revisor of Statutes: "* Miscellaneous taxes include corporate franchise taxes and fees."
