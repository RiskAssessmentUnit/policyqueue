Kansas tax revenue or STAR bonds are not mentioned in the provided evidence. However, the prices for Kansas Statutes Annotated (KSA) volumes and supplements have been established by the Legislative Coordinating Council (LCC).

According to the recommended prices for KSA, the bound volumes prices for 2024 and 2025 are $30 and $28, respectively. The supplements prices for 2024 and 2025 are $80 and $90, respectively. The supplements per volume prices for 2024 and 2025 are $8 and $10, respectively. The index prices for 2024 and 2025 are $16 and $20, respectively.

The LCC states that prices are established to "recover the actual costs of printing and binding such volumes." (Source: http://www.ksrevisor.org/docs/LCC_Packet20251013/Item_5_2026_KSA_prices_annotated.pdf)
