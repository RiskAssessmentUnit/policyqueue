**Budget Instructions to State Agencies**

The state's tax revenue has been a topic of interest in recent years. According to a report, "Kansas has the 5th lowest overall tax burden in the country, with a total tax burden of 8.9% of personal income" ("Kansas Tax Burden Ranks 5th Lowest in the Country," unknown source). Additionally, STAR bonds, a type of tax increment financing, have been used to fund projects such as the Kansas Speedway and the Overland Park Arboretum.

However, the use of STAR bonds has been criticized for being too lenient, with some arguing that it leads to "favoritism and inequitable treatment of special interests" ("STAR Bonds: A Review of the Kansas STAR Bond Program," unknown source).
