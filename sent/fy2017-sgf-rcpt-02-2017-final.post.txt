FY 2017 State General Fund Receipts

According to the Kansas State Treasurer's report, the state's total taxes for FY 2017 were $331,522,535. This includes corporate franchise taxes and fees, as mentioned in the report: "Miscellaneous taxes include corporate franchise taxes and fees."

The report also highlights that the state's total receipts for FY 2017 were $326,669,514.
