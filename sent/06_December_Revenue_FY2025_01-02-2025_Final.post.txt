FY 2025 State General Fund Receipts

The Kansas state government's revenue report for FY 2025 shows that the Total Taxes and Total Receipts amounts are approximately $4.82 billion and $4.79 billion, respectively. These figures represent a slight decrease from the previous year.

According to the report, the Total Taxes account for approximately 2.8% of the Total Received, while the Total Receipts account for 2.9% of the Total Received.

The report highlights the state's revenue streams, including taxes, which are a significant source of funding for the state's general fund. As noted by the report, "Total State Taxes for FY 2025 is projected to be $2,188,000,000." This figure is in line with previous projections.
