Revisions to State General Fund Receipts in Kansas

According to the Consensus Revenue Estimating Group, the revised estimates for State General Fund Receipts in FY 2016 were decreased by $98.2 million, or 1.7 percent below the November estimate. ("The revised estimate for FY 2016 of $5.713 billion was decreased by $98.2 million, or 1.7 percent below the November estimate.")

Additionally, the revised estimates for FY 2015 incorporate the fiscal effect of all 2015 legislation signed into law through April 20, 2015.
