FY 2023 State General Fund Receipts

According to the FY 2023 State General Fund Receipts Final Report: May 2023, "Total Taxes" reached $9,171,262,187 in FY 2023. The report also noted that "Total Receipts" for the same period totaled $8,236,366,750.

Additionally, the report broke down tax revenues by type, including $4,128,041,840 in individual income taxes for FY 2022, and $2,531,023,612 in sales and use taxes from retail sales for FY 2022.
