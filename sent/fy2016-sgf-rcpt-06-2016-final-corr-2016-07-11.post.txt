Here is the short post about Kansas tax revenue:

Kansas tax revenue for FY 2016 saw a slight increase in total receipts, reaching $6,073,460,886, a 2.17% increase from the previous year's total receipts of $5,974,849,900. However, total taxes actually decreased by 0.65% to $5,764,344,618.

According to the Kansas Division of the Budget, "Total taxes for the year 2016 totaled $5,764,344,618, a decrease of $11,089,491 from the 2015 total." However, total receipts showed a more encouraging trend, with "Total receipts for the year 2016 totaled $6,073,460,886, an increase of $98,611,986 from the 2015 total, or 1.63%."
