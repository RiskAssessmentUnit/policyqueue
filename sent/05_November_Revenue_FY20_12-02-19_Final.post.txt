FY 2020 State General Fund Receipts

According to the Kansas Department of Revenue, the state's total tax revenue for FY 2020 was $8.3 billion, with KDOR tax collections reaching a subtotal of $2.8 billion. This represents a significant increase from the previous year, with KDOR tax collections growing by $1.1 billion.

"The FY 2020 state general fund receipts reflect an increase of 4.2% compared to FY 2019." 

"It's worth noting that KDOR tax collections account for a significant portion of the state's total revenue."
