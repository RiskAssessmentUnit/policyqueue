2025 Session Document Packet Service Report

The 2025 session document packet service report reveals revenue collected in the amount of $3,934.00, according to the report. "34 packets were distributed and/or purchased by state agencies and the public with revenue collected in the amount of $3,934.00." [1]

It's worth noting that the report also includes pricing information for document packets, including a price of $544.00 with tax and $524.00 tax-exempt. Additionally, the document room pick-up price with tax is $219.90, and tax-exempt is $200.00.
