FY 2016 State General Fund Receipts Final Report: April 2016

The Kansas state government's total tax revenue for FY 2016 was $4,713,402,533, with a total of $4,589,876,312 in tax revenue after adjustments. This represents a slight increase of $2,592,533, or 0.06%, from the previous year.

As noted in the report, "Miscellaneous taxes now include corporate franchise taxes and fees." (Source: Report date, May 2, 2016)
