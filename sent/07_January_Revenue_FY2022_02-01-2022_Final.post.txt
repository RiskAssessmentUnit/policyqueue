FY 2022 State General Fund Receipts

The Kansas Division of the Budget reported that the state's total taxes and total receipts reached $940,116,526 and $807,170,000, respectively, in FY 2022. This represents a 14.5% and 16.3% increase in total taxes and total receipts compared to the previous year.

"It's a positive trend," said the Kansas Division of the Budget. "The state's economy is growing, and that's resulting in more tax revenue coming in."
