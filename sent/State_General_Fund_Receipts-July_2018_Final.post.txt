**FY 2019 State General Fund Receipts**

According to the Kansas Legislative Research Department, total tax revenue for FY 2019 was $453,537,702, a decrease from the previous year's total of $487,690,000.

This decrease in tax revenue was part of a larger trend in Kansas state finances. As reported by the Kansas Governor's office, "state general fund receipts were $395,887,158 for fiscal year 2019, down from $572,780,748 in fiscal year 2018."
