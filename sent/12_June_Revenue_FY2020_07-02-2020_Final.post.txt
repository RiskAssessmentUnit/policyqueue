**FY 2020 State General Fund Receipts Final Report**

The Kansas Department of Revenue (KDOR) has released its final report on state general fund receipts for FY 2020. According to the report, total taxes collected were $7,049,143,310.

Breaking down the numbers, we see that "other taxes" accounted for $182,947,586, while subtotal - KDOR tax collections reached $6,866,195,724.

Notably, a change in reporting was made in July 2020, with corporate franchise taxes now listed separately from miscellaneous taxes. "Corporate franchise is now listed separately from miscellaneous," according to the report.
