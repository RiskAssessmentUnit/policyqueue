FY 2018 State General Fund Receipts
Type: REVENUE_REPORT

According to the Kansas Department of Revenue, the state's total taxes for FY 2018 were $6,288,970,329. This included $5,615,487,533 in KDOR tax collections and $656,300,131 in total receipts.

Miscellaneous taxes, which include corporate franchise taxes and fees, contributed to the state's revenue. As noted, "Miscellaneous taxes include corporate franchise taxes and fees."

The breakdown of tax revenue for FY 2018 includes:

* Property tax/fee: $594,844
* Income taxes: individual: $271,059,243
* Excise taxes: retail sales: $189,274,279
