FY 2022 State General Fund Receipts

According to the Kansas Division of the Budget, the state's total taxes for FY 2022 were $3,378,670,000. ("Final Report: November 2021")

In terms of overall receipts, Kansas received $5,688,224,437 in FY 2022. This was slightly lower than the $6,372,760,193 in receipts for FY 2021.

It's worth noting that the state's tax revenue includes various components, including income tax, sales tax, and other revenues. The Kansas Department of Revenue reported $3,385,683,176 in KDOR tax collections for FY 2022.
