Kansas FY 2019 State General Fund Receipts

According to the latest data, the total taxes received by the state was $1,698,239,805, accounting for 11.39% of the total receipts. This includes $695,875,721 from total taxes, with $695,153,620 from KDOR tax collections.

It's worth noting that miscellaneous taxes, which include corporate franchise taxes and fees, were reported to be $140,190,000 in FY 2019. This figure aligns with previous reports, which stated that miscellaneous taxes had been "$141,302,249" in FY 2018.
