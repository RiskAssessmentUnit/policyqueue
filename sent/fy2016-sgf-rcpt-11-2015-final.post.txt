Here is a short post about Kansas tax revenue:

The Kansas Department of Revenue (KDOR) reported that the state's general fund receipts for FY 2016 totaled $2,227,788,001. This total includes "Subtotal - KDOR Tax Collections" of $2,238,160,000, which is the largest component of state revenue. However, "Other Taxes" actually decreased by $2,250,000.
