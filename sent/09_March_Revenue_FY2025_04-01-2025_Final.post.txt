FY 2025 State General Fund Receipts Final Report: March 2025

According to the report, the state's total tax revenue for FY 2025 reached $6,948,911,667. The breakdown of tax collections shows that individual income taxes accounted for $3,205,288,614, while corporate income taxes contributed $758,744,295. The report also notes that financial institutions' revenue was $28,896,236.

In terms of STAR bonds, the report highlights that the state's total receipts were $6,986,213,149, which includes interest on investments. Notably, the report states that "total receipts exceeded projections, with a significant increase in tax revenue." However, there is no specific mention of STAR bonds.
