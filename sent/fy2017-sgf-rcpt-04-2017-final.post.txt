**FY 2017 State General Fund Receipts**

According to the Kansas Department of Revenue, the state's total tax revenue for FY 2017 was $639,215,710. This represents a significant portion of the state's overall revenue, with total receipts reaching $738,046,421.

As noted in the FY 2017 annual report, "KDOR Tax Collections" made up the largest share of total taxes, with a value of $636,637,670. Other significant contributors to state revenue included insurance premiums ($2,578,040) and miscellaneous taxes ($2,165,801).
