FY 2021 State General Fund Receipts Final Report: December 2020

According to the report, the state's general fund receipts for FY 2021 saw a significant increase. The dollar change from the previous year was "$1,342,084" (Report).

Revenue from general fund sources reached "$306,676,400" in FY 2021, with a percent change of 11.8% compared to the previous year (Report).

The report also noted that revenue from STAR bonds contributed to the overall increase in state tax revenue.
