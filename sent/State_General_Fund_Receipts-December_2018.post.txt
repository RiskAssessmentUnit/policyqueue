Title: FY 2019 State General Fund Receipts Final Report: December 2018

The Kansas state government released its FY 2019 State General Fund Receipts Final Report for December 2018. According to the report, the total taxes received were $136,106,074. This represents a 2.84% increase from the previous year.

A notable aspect of the report is the mention of STAR bonds, which are tax-exempt revenue bonds used to finance sports stadiums and other economic development projects. The report notes that the state received $5,000,000 in STAR bond payments, which is a relatively small portion of the total taxes received.

The report highlights the importance of understanding the financial impact of STAR bonds on the state's general fund. "The use of STAR bonds can have a significant impact on state revenues, as they are exempt from state and local taxes," said a state official.
