FY 2018 State General Fund Receipts Final Report: December 2017

According to the report, total taxes collected in Kansas for FY 2018 was $3,181,866,290. This includes $3,121,579,722 in KDOR tax collections, $70,306,568 in insurance premiums, and $702,965,568 in miscellaneous taxes, which include corporate franchise taxes and fees ("Miscellaneous taxes include corporate franchise taxes and fees."). Other notable revenue sources include $8,180,642 in interest and $170,346,172 in net transfers and other receipts.
