**Kansas Certified Population**

As of July 1, 2021, the certified population of Kansas was announced by the Division of the Budget. The population growth in Kansas from 2018 to 2019 was 1.0% ("% Chg 2018-2019", value: 1.0, unit: PERCENT, year: 2019), and from 2019 to 2020 was 2.5% ("% Chg 2019-2020", value: 2.5, unit: PERCENT, year: 2020).
