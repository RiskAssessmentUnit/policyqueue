FY 2023 State General Fund Receipts Final Report: September 2022

According to the Kansas Division of the Budget, the state's total tax revenue for FY 2023 reached a record high. The final report states, "$970,000,000 in dollar change." This represents a significant increase of 12.7% compared to the previous year.

The total taxes collected for FY 2023 were $234,934,970.

It's worth noting that the state's STAR bonds, a financing tool for large infrastructure projects, have been a topic of discussion in recent years. The use of STAR bonds has been criticized for diverting tax revenue away from the state's general fund.
