FY 2021 State General Fund Receipts Final Report: April 2021

The final report on state general fund receipts for FY 2021 indicates that total taxes received were $982,383,022. This is part of the larger total receipts, which reached $1,028,897,472. 

It's worth noting that a portion of the state's revenue comes from STAR bonds, which "are not subject to state income tax" and "are subject to a tax on the gross income from the sale of the bonds" ("$319,921,616"). However, this specific amount is not mentioned in the report. Another relevant figure is $285,013,192, but its relation to STAR bonds or tax revenue is unclear.
