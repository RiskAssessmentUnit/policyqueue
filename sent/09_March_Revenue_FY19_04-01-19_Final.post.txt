FY 2019 State General Fund Receipts

The State of Kansas' general fund receipts for FY 2019 totaled $4,913,672,015, with $4,826,854,615 of that coming from KDOR tax collections.

According to the Kansas Legislative Research Department, "KDOR tax collections represent the majority of total state general fund receipts." ("KDOR tax collections represent the majority of total state general fund receipts.")

The report also notes that "miscellaneous taxes include corporate franchise taxes and fees."
