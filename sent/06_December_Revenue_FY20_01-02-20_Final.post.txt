FY 2020 State General Fund Receipts Final Report: December 2019

According to the report, the total taxes collected in FY 2020 was $3,580,885,324, which is a $50,875,324 increase from the previous year. The subtotal of KDOR tax collections was $3,508,237,281. 

The report notes that "the state's tax collections have shown steady growth over the past year."
