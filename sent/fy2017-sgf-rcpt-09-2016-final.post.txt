According to the FY 2017 State General Fund Receipts Final Report: September 2016, total taxes collected by the state of Kansas reached $521,186,520 in 2017. This represents a decrease of $44,703,480 from the previous year, or a 7.90% decline.

The report notes that "KDOR Tax Collections" accounted for the majority of tax revenue, totaling $521,008,945. Within this category, property tax/fee: motor carrier accounted for $583,329, while income taxes: individual generated $205,808,223, and excise taxes: retail sales generated $190,589,829.

It is worth noting that the report also highlights that certain tax categories experienced significant decreases, including a $112,424 drop in "excise taxes: retail sales" resulting in a 38.77% decrease from the previous year.
