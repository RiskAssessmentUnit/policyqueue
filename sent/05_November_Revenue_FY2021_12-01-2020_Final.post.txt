FY 2021 State General Fund Receipts

According to the Kansas revenue report, the state received a total of $5,567,603,306 in FY 2021. This represents a 1.1% increase from the previous year's total of $5,440,113,888. The report notes that "Total taxes received by the State General Fund for FY 2021 were $2,798,900,450." Additionally, the report states that "Total receipts for FY 2021 were $2,811,964,850."
